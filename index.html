THE JOURNEY OF A CUP OF TEA

A CUP OF TEA It is said that if you conquer Pune, you can conquer the world. 'Yewale Chaha' has won Pune's heart and is now set to win over not only Maharashtra but India and the World’s hearts. It is our dream to serve Yewale Chaha to tea lovers across the globe, for which we have taken a big leap and now are planning for a giant one. The entire next generation of the Yewale clan is striving to make Yewale Tea a world-class brand with the best quality and service. The secret behind Yewale Amruttulya’s growth and success lies in its unmatched taste, credit for which goes to the Yewale family and their dedication to devising a recipe that ensures a similar taste at all of their branches. This has helped the brand establish impeccable planning, meticulous management and best service throughout the branches.

QUALITY IS DEFINITELY OUR CUP OF TEA

We strongly believe that making good tea is no joke! It is a culinary art that requires patience, knowledge of locally sourced ingredients, their quality and strategic decisions regarding alterations of recipes. From hygiene to the quality of tea powder, the balance of ingredients to the amount of sugar, everything is dealt with precision and perfection.

JOIN HANDS WITH YEWALE TEA
